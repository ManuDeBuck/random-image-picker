Explanation

Above you can choose pictures you want to add to the carousel by clicking 'Browse'. When clicking on 'Next Step' you wil get to the random-image-picker screen. There you can click on 'Pick random image' to pick a random image. Afterwards it will be removed from the queue and you can click the button again. Now it will pick another random image and the previous image will be excluded. You can also reset the images and upload new ones by clicking the 'Reset images button'.
